I'm ready to serve your every need. I'll plan the detailed itinerary and provide transportation. All you need to do is wear suitable shoes and clothes, and be prepared to explore.

BIGHORN SHEEP SPOTTING

On this 5 mile loop in the Sand to Snow National Monument you are almost guaranteed to see desert bighorn sheep. This is a strenuous hike on a steep narrow trail that is not maintained. If you have a fear of heights this is not the hike for you. With a 1500 foot gain and following a narrow ridge the trail is challenging. There is also opportunity to see mule deer, coyotes, jackrabbits, bobcats and antelope squirrel. With views of the Coachella Valley and the San Bernardino Mountains this is a spectacular hike. After the hike you have the option of enjoying a picnic lunch in the shade of mature cottonwoods.

CACTUS TO CLOUDS

Looking to experience breathtaking scenery on one of the most challenging hikes in North America? If you are, then this trek is for you. This is only for serious hikers as you will gain 8000 feet in the first 9 miles. Then an additional 2000 feet in the next 6 miles to San Jacinto Peak. The descent is 5.5 miles to the tram which you ride to desert floor. At just over 20 miles this is an all day event and not for the feint of heart.

There is an option to hike from the valley floor to the tram. 8000 feet in 9 miles. Contact me for details.

Open your mind to a new challenge. Interested in booking? Get in touch to learn more now.

SAN JACINTO PEAK

At 10,833 feet San Jacinto is the second tallest mountain in Southern California. After a short tram ride you will be in tall pines and firs, much cooler temperatures, and far removed from the desert. The trail gains 2,300 feet in 5.5 miles. This in and out hike is 11 miles and is considered strenuous. There are options to picnic at the peak or dine at the top of the tram. I am here to take care of all of the details, from planning the perfect itinerary, to showing you a unique and memorable adventure.

HIDDEN SLOT CANYONS

There are a few options ranging from 3-6 miles. Each is unique and you will be mesmerized by the twists and turns in these narrow canyons. You must be able to climb up and down ladders to participate in these hikes.

On the drive to the trailhead we will pass date farms and views of the Salton Sea.

There are options for picnicing along the trail or stopping for sandwiches and date shakes on the drive home. I'm here to customize the trek any way you like. The sky's the limit.
